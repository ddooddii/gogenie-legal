# GoGenie Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-07._

GoGenie helps you fix everyday iPhone problems. This policy explains, in plain
words, what the app does and does not do with your information. If anything here
is unclear, email **ddooddii@gmail.com**.

## The short version

- GoGenie works without an account. Most of what it knows about you stays on
  your phone.
- When you talk to Genie (or share your screen during a Genie Call), what you
  send is passed to our Genie service to generate a reply. We do not sell your
  data, show ads, or use your conversations to train advertising profiles.
- You can use the app anonymously, and you can delete everything by deleting the
  app.

## What stays on your device

- Your name (if you share it), the issues you pick, your progress through the
  guided chapters, and Genie's memory of past sessions are stored **locally** on
  your iPhone. They are not uploaded unless you send them to Genie in a message.
- Voice notes you record are saved temporarily on your device and are not
  uploaded.

## What is sent to our Genie service

When you send a message to Genie or end a Genie Call, the app sends:

- The text of your conversation turn, and a short summary of what Genie
  remembers about you (so replies stay relevant).
- If you used Genie Call, a small number of still frames captured from your
  screen recording, so Genie can see what you were looking at.
- An anonymous device identifier used only to prevent abuse and apply your free
  trial.

This information is processed to generate Genie's reply and is not used for
advertising or sold to anyone.

### Who processes it

- **Anthropic** (the Claude API) generates Genie's replies. Your message text and
  any shared screen frames are sent to Anthropic for that purpose.
- **Cloudflare** hosts the Genie service that routes your request.
- **Resend** sends the sign-in code email, if you choose to create an email
  account (your email address only).

We do not send your information to advertisers or data brokers.

## Accounts (optional)

If you sign in with Apple or create an email account, we store the account
identifier (and, for email, your email address) so Genie can recognize you when
you return. Your help history currently stays on your phone; syncing it between
devices is planned for a future version. You can use GoGenie fully without an
account.

## Data retention

- On-device data lives until you delete it (or delete the app).
- Conversation requests are processed to produce a reply and are not retained by
  us to build a profile. Sign-in codes expire within 10 minutes.

## Children

GoGenie is a general-audience utility and is not directed at children under 13.

## Your choices

- Use the app anonymously (skip sign-in).
- Delete the app to remove all on-device data.
- Contact **ddooddii@gmail.com** to ask about account deletion.

## Changes

We'll update this policy as the product changes and note the date at the top.
